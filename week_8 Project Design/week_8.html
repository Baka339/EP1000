Project Design

For my final project i wanted to do a simple present that people could give to their friends or loved ones. Its suppose to be simple enough to fix and also to be customisable. Below are the sketchs that I drew while thinking about my final product. There were minor changes to the end product but my sketch helped me with an idea to start with.

After finishing my final sketch that I wanted, I took to fusion 360 to start designing the box. Had to take some measurements because I wanted to add a music player inside and a small circuit box too so that when you open that box it would light up. The box will consist of 2 compartments the bottom mainly to hold the circuit while also acting as a sercert space to keep some small personal items while the top is the main compartment to store the main stuff. Also I 3d printed a hinge for the box so it would open and close.
